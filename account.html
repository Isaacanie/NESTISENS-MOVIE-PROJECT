Filmpire Accounts

Welcome to Filmpire! Your Filmpire account gives you access to a world of movies, TV shows, and exclusive content. Below, you'll find everything you need to know about creating, managing, and securing your account.

1. Creating a Filmpire Account

To start streaming, you need to sign up for an account:

    ✅ Visit www.filmpire.com/signup.
    ✅ Enter your email address and create a secure password.
    ✅ Choose a subscription plan (if required).
    ✅ Complete the verification process.
    ✅ Start watching your favorite content!

📌 Note: Some content may be available for free, while premium content requires a subscription.

2. Managing Your Account

    🔹 Edit Profile: Update your name, email, and password.
    🔹 Subscription & Billing: View or change your plan, update payment methods.
    🔹 Watch History & Preferences: Customize your recommendations.
    🔹 Device Management: See all devices logged into your account and manage access.

📌 Access your account settings at: www.filmpire.com/account.

3. Account Security & Privacy

🔐 Protecting Your Account:

  • Use a strong password with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) if available.
  • Avoid sharing your login details with others.
  • Regularly review logged-in devices and sign out from unrecognized ones.
    • 📌 Forgot Password? Reset it easily at www.filmpire.com/reset-password.

4. Multiple Profiles & Parental Controls

    👥 Create Up to [X] Profiles: Customize viewing preferences for each family member.
    🔹 Kids Mode: Restrict access to age-appropriate content.
    🔹 PIN Protection: Secure certain profiles with a PIN.

📌 Set up profiles in your account settings: www.filmpire.com/profiles.

5. Deleting or Deactivating Your Account

If you wish to delete your account permanently:

    ✅ Go to Account Settings > Delete Account.
    ✅ Confirm your request (deletion is irreversible).
    ✅ Your personal data will be removed in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

📌 Need help? Contact support@filmpire.com for assistance.
