Who are we

Filmpire is a streaming entertainment service that provides a wide variety of TV shows, movies, documentaries, and other content on-demand to subscribers worldwide. Founded in 1997 by Reed Hastings and Marc Randolph, Filmpire initially started as a DVD rental service, where customers could order DVDs online and receive them through the mail. However, in 2007, it shifted to streaming, and this pivot transformed it into one of the most popular entertainment platforms globally.
Filmpire has also influenced the entertainment industry by introducing binge-watching, where entire seasons of shows are released at once, allowing viewers to watch at their own pace. Today, Filmpire is one of the biggest names in the streaming industry, competing with other major players like Disney+, Amazon Prime Video, and HBO Max.
