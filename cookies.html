At Filmpire, we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, improve our services, and provide personalized content. This policy explains how we use cookies and how you can manage your preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website or use our app. They help us recognize you, save your preferences, and improve your streaming experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

Filmpire uses cookies for the following purposes:

    🔹 Essential Cookies – Required for site functionality, such as logging in and secure transactions.
    🔹 Performance Cookies – Help us analyze how users interact with Filmpire to improve performance.
    🔹 Functional Cookies – Store user preferences like language, subtitles, and theme settings.
    🔹 Advertising & Marketing Cookies – Deliver personalized recommendations and relevant ads.

3. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage or disable cookies by:

    ✅ Adjusting your browser settings (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
    ✅ Clicking on "Cookie Settings" in your Filmpire account.
    ✅ Using the "Opt-Out" feature for marketing and tracking cookies.

🚨 Note: Disabling certain cookies may impact your streaming experience, such as login functionality or video recommendations.

We may use third-party services (e.g., Google Analytics, advertising partners) to collect data for performance analysis and personalized ads. These partners have their own cookie policies, which you can review on their websites.
